Mother Goose Special Science High School

The Mother Goose Playschool and Gradeschool are two of several schools that

part of the Mother Goose Special Schools System in the Philippines.

The main school is in Dagupan. There are also branches in Pasig, Urdaneta,

Bayambang, Pangasinan, U.P. Village, Quezon City, Marikina, San Carlos, Pangasinan, and Makati.


Like all great undertakings, the establishment of Mother Goose Playskool (MGP) started as a

vision in the heart and mind of its founder, the late Dr. Luis F. Samson. Daddy Luis as he is

known to the family was a self-made man, an icon among his peers and a symbol of excellence

and integrity in the community that he served. His greatest passion was education such that the

she founded (MGP and University of Luzon) rank as two of the best providers of competitive education

in the entire country today.
